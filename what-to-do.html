What to do?

Explore and have in Taniti!

There is so much to do in Taniti. Explore Attractions and more...

Taniti is home to beautiful sights, outdoor adventures, and magnificent beaches. Whether you’re admiring one of the island’s many waterfalls, hiking a scenic trail, or simply relaxing, we know you'll find your perfect experience.

There are so many things to see and great places to visit on Taniti Island that choosing which ones are right for you and your travel party can seem a bit overwhelming. There are so many questions: Which beaches are near where I'm staying? Which waterfalls are worth my time? Where are the best places for a Manta Ray tour? Where are the top Big Island hiking trails? What should I see in Taniti National Park? In this section, you'll find helpful articles about some of the best and most popular Hawaiian attractions to help you plan your perfect Taniti vacation. Enjoy!
